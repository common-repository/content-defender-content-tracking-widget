Content Defender Widget for WordPress keeps track of your digital content syndication and distribution around the web so you don't have to.

== Description ==

Content Defender is a digital content discovery service that keeps track of the syndication of your content.  We scour the web keeping track of your content and reporting back to you on where it can be found.

No registration is necessary for installation and to begin tracking, but registration is required to receive reports on a weekly basis.  Registration is free, we just need an email to notify you about new reports.

Register at : [http://contentdefender.com]

== Installation ==

1. Upload `content_defender_widget.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place `<?php do_action('plugin_name_hook'); ?>` in your templates
